## Heatmap Correlation 

The heatmap shows the correlation between key laptop hardware features,
highlighting relationships across performance, display, and pricing attributes.

### Key takeways: 
- SSD capacity and RAM show the strongest correlation with price compare to any other features,
- Screen size is highly correlated with laptop weight,
- Higher display resolution is highly correlated with PPI which in by the data could be associated with higher-priced devices.
